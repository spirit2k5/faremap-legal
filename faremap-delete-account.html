Delete a FareMap Account

Use this page to request deletion even when FareMap is not installed.

Delete inside FareMap

  1. Open FareMap and sign in.
  2. Open My Profile.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Type DELETE and confirm permanent deletion.

Request deletion without the app

Email FareMap support from the address connected to your account. Include your FareMap account email and clearly request permanent deletion.

Email deletion request

Support: mahloricarlton@gmail.com

What is deleted

FareMap will delete or anonymise the authentication account and associated FareMap data, including profile information, role, votes, confirmations, favourites, budgets, waiting requests, live-driver records and other account-linked records, unless limited retention is required for security, fraud prevention or legal obligations.

What to expect

  • FareMap may verify that you own the account.
  • Deletion requests will normally be completed within 30 days.
  • You do not need to reinstall FareMap to request deletion.
  • After deletion, deleted account data cannot be restored.
