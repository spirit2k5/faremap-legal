Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 July 2026

This policy explains how FareMap handles account information, location information and community contributions.

Who operates FareMap

FareMap is operated by Carlton Mahlori Tshianeo in South Africa. Contact mahloricarlton@gmail.com with privacy questions.

Information FareMap may process

  • Account information: email address, display name, Supabase user ID and selected Passenger or Driver role.
  • Location information: foreground location used when a passenger selects a waiting point, opens a live map feature, or when a driver deliberately goes Online.
  • Community information: fares, routes, places, pickup points, reports, votes, confirmations and waiting requests.
  • App activity: records needed for favourites, budgets, requests, fraud prevention and account security.

How information is used

FareMap uses information to authenticate users, provide Passenger and Driver tools, display transport information, protect one-vote and one-confirmation rules, prevent abuse, maintain security and diagnose technical problems.

Location

FareMap is designed to use location only when a user deliberately starts a location-based feature. Drivers share live location while Online. Passengers share a waiting location only after choosing a public waiting point. FareMap does not intend to collect background location while the app is closed.

Service providers

FareMap uses Supabase for authentication, database, cloud and server-function services. Google authentication is used when a user chooses Google login.

Sharing and sale

FareMap does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that operate FareMap, disclosed when legally required, or displayed to other users when intentionally submitted as public community information.

Security

FareMap uses authenticated accounts, encrypted connections and Supabase access controls. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

Retention

Live driver and passenger waiting information is kept only as long as needed for the active feature. Account and community records may remain until deleted, anonymised or no longer needed for FareMap, security, fraud prevention or legal obligations.

Your choices and account deletion

You can stop sharing live location by going Offline or ending a waiting request. You can request permanent deletion through My Profile → Delete Account inside FareMap or from the public deletion page.

Policy changes

This policy may be updated when FareMap features, providers or legal requirements change. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are published.
