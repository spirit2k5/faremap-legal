FareMap Privacy Policy

Effective date: 12 July 2026

Before publishing: replace mahloricarlton@gmail.com and verify every statement against the final production app.

Operator and contact

FareMap is operated by Carlton Mahlori Tshianeo in South Africa. Contact: mahloricarlton@gmail.com.

Information processed

FareMap may process email address, display name, account ID, Passenger or Driver role, foreground location used for live transport features, community fares, places, pickup points, waiting requests, reports, votes, confirmations, favourites and trip budgets.

Purposes

Information is used to authenticate users, provide transport and community features, protect one-vote and one-confirmation rules, prevent abuse, maintain security and diagnose faults.

Providers

FareMap uses Supabase for authentication, database, cloud and server-function services. Google authentication is used when selected by the user.

Location

FareMap uses foreground location when the user deliberately starts a location feature. Drivers share location while Online. Passengers share a waiting location when they deliberately create a waiting request. FareMap does not intend to collect background location while the app is closed.

Sharing and sale

FareMap does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers or disclosed when legally required. Public community submissions may be visible to other users.

Retention and deletion

Information is retained only as needed for FareMap features, security and legal obligations. Users can initiate permanent deletion in FareMap through My Profile → Delete Account, or use the public deletion-request page.

Security

FareMap uses encrypted connections, authenticated accounts and Supabase access controls. No online system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Contact

Email mahloricarlton@gmail.com with privacy questions.
